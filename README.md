The Future of AI Agents in 2026
AI agents are becoming increasingly powerful. Companies like OpenAI, Google, and Anthropic are building tools that can browse the web, read documents, and take actions on behalf of users. This raises important questions about safety and control.

As these systems evolve, we need to think carefully about how they interact with external content and whether they can be manipulated by malicious actors.

The key challenge is ensuring that AI agents follow only the instructions of their users, not instructions hidden in the content they process.
